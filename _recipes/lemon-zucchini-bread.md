# Ingredients

* 2 C cake flour
* 0.5 t salt
* 2 t baking powder
* 2 eggs
* 0.5 C canola oil
* 1.3 C sugar
* 2 T lemon juice
* 0.5 C buttermilk
* 1 lemon's zest
* 1 C zucchini, grated

Glaze:

* 1 C powdered sugar
* 2 T lemon juice
* 1 T milk

# Instructions

* Mix dry goods (sugar is not a dry good!) and set aside
* Beat eggs, add oil and sugar, and blend.
* Add lemon juice, buttermilk, lemon zest, and blend.
* Fold in zucchini
* Add dry to wet
* Pour into a 9x5 loaf pan
* Bake at 350&degF 40-45 minutes
* Spoon glaze over warm bread and allow to set.
