by Stacy

* 1-1/4 cup oatbran
* 1 cup oatmeal
* 1 Tbsp baking powder
* 1/2 tsp cinnamon
* 1/4 tsp all spice
* 1/4 tsp nutmeg
* 3/4 cup dried or fresh cranberries (or raisins)
* 3/4 cup walnut or pecan pieces
* 1/4 honey or brown sugar
* 14 oz. unsweetened applesauce (organic is best, or [[Recipe:Applesauce|make your own]])
* 2 egg whites
* 2 Tbsp oil (I use olive oil)
* 1/4 cup soy milk (I use vanilla) 

Sometines I also add one small, diced apple

# Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
# Mix dry ingredients.
# Add remaining ingredients and mix well.
# Adjust consistency to a thick batter by adding more oatmeal or soymilk.
# Spoon into greased muffin tins.
# Bake at 425 degress for about 15 minutes.
