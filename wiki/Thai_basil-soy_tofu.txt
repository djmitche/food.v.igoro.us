[[Category:Staples|Thai basil-soy tofu]]
[[Category:Ingredient:Tofu|Thai basil-soy tofu]]
=Ingredients=
* 2 cakes tofu (firm)
* 0.5 C soy
* 2/3 C veggie stock or water
* 2 T lime juice
* 1 T minced ginger
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1 t sambal oelek
* 2 oz palm sugar
* 0.25 C chopped Thai basil
=Instructions=
* slice the tofu into half-height slabs
* simmer everything but the basil and the tofu
* add the basil and cover the tofu
* marinate 8 hours
