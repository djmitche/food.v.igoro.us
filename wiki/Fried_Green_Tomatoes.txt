Like many southern recipes, this is so easy as to be trivial.  But (again, like many southern recipes) it tastes great.

=Ingredients=
* Green Tomatoes
* Cornmeal
* Oil or butter

=Instructions=
# Slice the tomatoes to 1/4
# Dip them in the cornmeal (dip them in water first, or even egg, if they're dry)
# Fry 'em in a shallow pan with ample oil
# Soak on paper towels
